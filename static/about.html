About DevOps Study Planner

The DevOps Study Planner is a lightweight web tool designed to help aspiring DevOps engineers track their learning journey and stay focused. Whether you're self-learning, taking a course, or preparing for job interviews, this planner helps you stay consistent and goal-oriented.

It breaks down essential DevOps topics—from Linux and Git to Kubernetes, Terraform, and AWS—into a structured checklist with progress tracking and dark mode support. All progress is saved locally in your browser, so you can pick up where you left off at any time.
